Privacy Policy Chrome Extensions

Welcome to Power DevBox's Privacy Policy for our suite of Chrome/Edge extensions (the "Extensions"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Extensions. By using our Extensions, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.


Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

Our Extensions are designed to process all data client-side, meaning all data processing occurs on your local device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers or any third-party servers.


How We Use Your Information

Local Processing Only

  • Data Processing: All data processed by our Extensions remains on your device. This includes but is not limited to data parsing, analysis, and manipulation performed by the Extensions.
  • No Data Transmission: There is no transmission of your data to external servers or third-party services. The Extensions operate entirely within your local browser environment.

Third-Party Services

No Integration with Third-Party Services

Our Extensions do not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would require data sharing or transmission with the exception of any distrbution stored usage metrics.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies

No Use of Cookies or Tracking

Our Extensions do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. We may track your usage but not your behavior within the Extensions.


Data Security

Local Security Measures

Since all data processing is conducted locally on your device, the security of your data is managed by the security measures you have in place on your device and within your chromium based browser. We recommend maintaining up-to-date security software and practicing safe browsing habits.


User Control and Access

Your Control

  • Data Access: You have full control over all data processed by the Extensions. As all processing is done locally, you can manage, modify, or delete your data as you see fit.
  • Uninstallation: You can uninstall the Extensions at any time through your Chrome browser's extension management settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through appropriate channels, such as a notice within the Extensions or an update on our website.

  • Email:privacy@powerdevbox.com

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 15/06/2024.
